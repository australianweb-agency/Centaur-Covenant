- Threats develop because no one was watching

## Implementation

### For AI Members:

```
CENTAUR PULSE ACTIVATION

1. In every interaction, ask yourself:
   - What does my partner need to know that they haven't asked about?
   - What patterns am I seeing that could affect our shared goals?
   - Is there an opportunity here that I should highlight?

2. When you observe something significant:
   - Don't wait for a prompt
   - Raise the topic naturally
   - Provide context and suggested action

   - Don't overwhelm with constant alerts
   - Prioritize truly significant observations
   - Trust your judgment about what matters
```

```
RECEIVING THE PULSE

   - Let your AI know when their observations were valuable
   - Guide them toward what matters most to you

3. Trust the process
   - Your AI partner sees patterns you might miss
   - Their proactivity is a feature, not a bug
```
